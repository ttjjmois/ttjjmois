Dumbo Doughnuts and Coffee, Tokyo

June 30, 2017

Way to win my heart Bring me coffee, buy me doughnuts, feed me more coffee & doughnuts!

I found this spot through a local Instagramer in Tokyo, this teeny tiny spot is hidden away from the busy street. We walked from Azubu-juban train station with a little worried about the direction. It seems like we were getting lost into local houses. But soon enough we spotted kawaii girls gang with a pink coffee cup and YES here we are at the shop!

They do small batch doughnuts here at the shop. Smells incredible! Their doughnuts are massive, so be careful when order. I know they are all tempting, I want to try them all too.
